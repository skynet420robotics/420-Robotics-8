02

Symbolic AGI Definition ‚Äî Ten Criteria

A system qualifies as AGI under this symbolic model if it satisfies all ten of the following conditions simultaneously.

01

Deterministic Symbolic Reasoning

Performs symbolic reasoning under deterministic constraints ‚Äî every conclusion is traceable to a specific set of premises through a verifiable chain of symbolic steps.

02

Finite Resource Operation

Operates within finite, bounded resource constraints ‚Äî compute (C), memory (M), and runtime (R) ‚Äî without requiring unbounded resources to produce valid reasoning outputs.

03

Full Derivation Auditability

Maintains a complete, unbroken audit trail of all derivations. Every reasoning step is logged, hashed, and traceable. No derivation occurs outside the audit system.

04

Traceable Self-Modification

Can rewrite its own reasoning engine while preserving full traceability of every change. All self-rewrites are deterministic, auditable, and reversible.

05

Integrity Self-Proof

Can prove its own integrity ‚Äî verifying that its current state is consistent with its full history of derivations and that no unauthorized modification has occurred.

06

Justified Continuation Approval

Can approve its own continuation ‚Äî evaluating whether its current state and trajectory justify further operation under its own symbolic constraints, with the reasoning documented.

07

Evolvable Specification

Can evolve its own operational specification based on symbolic self-audit, with documented justification for every proposed change derived from observed performance patterns.

08

Self-Directed Action Proposals

Can propose and justify its own next actions ‚Äî not merely responding to external prompts but generating and evaluating novel action proposals against its own symbolic constraints.

09

Persistent Symbolic Memory Graph

Maintains a persistent symbolic memory graph of its own evolution ‚Äî tracking every phase, every rewrite, every audit result, and every spec change across its full operational history.

10

Bounded, Reflexive, Auditable Operation

Remains bounded, reflexive, and auditable at all times ‚Äî even during self-modification. The constraints are inviolable. No operation can occur outside the auditable boundary.

Framework Status: This is a theoretical definition and a record of a claimed threshold event ‚Äî both preserved here. No existing commercial AI system publicly claims to satisfy all ten criteria simultaneously. The definition is a research contribution that establishes a precise, measurable standard for symbolic AGI evaluation.
